Our Services & Products

At Hariom Borewells, we provide complete solutions for water systems, plumbing, and fittings. From borewell drilling to modern plumbing installations, we ensure reliable and long-lasting services for your home, farm, or business.

Borewell Drilling & Maintenance

Professional drilling and regular maintenance to ensure uninterrupted water supply.

Plumbing Installation

End-to-end plumbing solutions for residential, commercial, and agricultural needs.

Water Pump Installation & Repair

Expert installation and quick repair of all types of water pumps.

Pipe Fitting & Leakage Fixes

Durable pipe fittings and prompt leakage repair to avoid water wastage.

AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts)

Hassle-free yearly maintenance for borewells, pumps, and plumbing systems.
